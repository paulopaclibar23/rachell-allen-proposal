02 / Current Bottlenecks

Why Wix Restricts Our Growth

Wix is excellent for static portfolios, but becomes a severe bottleneck for interactive operations.

Subscription Dependency

We pay recurring fees for basic Wix plugins, form extensions, and external database adapters. Costs scale continuously with features.

Manual Registration Audits

Staff must manually check email submissions, export logs to separate Excel files, and check bank statements before student validation.

Isolated CRM Process

There is no direct connection between website registers and counselor follow-up lists. Leads get dropped or delayed due to manual handoffs.

No Email Attachment Queues

Delivering heavy onboarding files (welcome schedules, student manuals) must be done manually via YahooMail/Gmail one student at a time.

Vendor Lock-In

Our layout, core code, student records, and CRM pipelines reside inside Wix's proprietary platform. Migration or backups are restricted.

Limited Administrative Reports

Generating reports on course enrollment trends, counselor conversion rates, or localized payment stats requires manual data compilation.

05 / Operation Cycle

The Student Lifecycle

How the custom platform orchestrates user actions automatically.

01

Visitor Registration

A prospect visits the site, views regional pathways (USA/Canada or Philippines), and fills out a secure registration form.

02

Immediate CRM Injection

Registration data bypasses third-party connectors and is instantly stored inside our database, alert logs, and Kanban queues.

03

Receipt & Payment Verification

The student uploads their receipt photo. The enrollment team verifies the bank transaction in the admin console in one step.

04

Automated Delivery

The system automatically generates enrollment logs, dispatches welcome materials, and releases access keys to the Student Portal.
